Our Story

Above the City,
Close to Your Heart

Perched on the rooftop of Centerpoint in the heart of Maijdee Court, Sky View Restaurant offers more than just a meal — it offers an experience. With sweeping views of Noakhali and a warm, inviting atmosphere, every visit feels like a special occasion.

We believe great food brings people together. Whether you are here for a family dinner, a quiet evening, or a celebration — Sky View is your place above it all.
